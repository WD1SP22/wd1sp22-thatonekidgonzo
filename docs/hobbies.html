Favorite Camping Spots

Destinations Ease of Access with car Highlights
Cherry Hill Very easily accessible There is a stream runnig through and it's pretty secluded yet close to the road.
Fort Bragg Most places are accessible once you hit the town. However some places along the way to Fort Bragg are recommended you have a 4X4 vehicle. The ocean is beautiful and there is a town near by to explore alongside Glass Beach.
Trinity Alps resort Cabins are rentable. If you drive past the resort there are free camping sites but 4X4 cars are strongly recommended. There is a river running along side the resort. Resort is open to anybody and there is a restaurant/bar and an icecream shop.
